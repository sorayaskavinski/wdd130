Overview

Purpose

To facilitate encounters between you and nature in a unique and profound way.

  • The water that resides in me greets the water that resides in you.
  • One thing we are is water! The body, the planet, the macro and the micro. 75% of everything is water.
  • This vital fluid is always in motion and action. Without water, life ends; water is life!

Audience

Our focus is to provide unique experiences in nature tourism and adventure for all people, including people with physical disabilities.

  • Q: I can't swim. Will I be able to do rafting?
    A: Yes, you can do rafting. Our team provides life jackets to everyone in the boat.
  • Q: Are there different scenarios to do the rafting?
    A: Yes, that's why we offer our service even to disabled people. There are three levels of adventure: beginner, medium, and adventurous.
  • Q: Is there a height/weight limit?
    A: There's no height limit. The weight limit is 120kg.
