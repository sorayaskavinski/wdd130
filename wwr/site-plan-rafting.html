Purpose

To facilitate encounters between you and nature in a unique and profound way. The water that resides in me greets the water that resides in you. One thing we are is water! The body, the planet, the macro and the micro. 75% of everything is water. This vital fluid is always in motion and action. Without water, life ends; water is life!

Audience

Our focus is to provide unique experiences in nature turism and adventure for all people, including people with physical disability
